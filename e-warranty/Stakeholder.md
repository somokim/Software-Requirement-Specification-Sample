## Stakeholder List

| ID | Role | Background | Primary Concerns | Relationship to System |
|----|------|------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| S01 | Customer / Product Owner | End consumer who has purchased a product and needs to register, track, or claim a warranty | Easy registration process, fast claim resolution, real-time status visibility, clear communication | Primary end-user of the customer-facing portal |
| S02 | Warranty Claims Processor | Internal staff member responsible for reviewing, validating, and actioning warranty claims | Efficient claims queue management, clear approval/rejection workflow, accurate customer data | Daily operational user of the claims processing module |
| S03 | Warranty Operations Manager | Manages the warranty department, oversees staff workload, SLAs, and process compliance | Reduced processing time, staff productivity, SLA adherence, exception handling | Supervisory user; monitors dashboards and approves escalated claims |
| S04 | Customer Service Representative | Front-line support agent who assists customers with warranty enquiries and escalations | Quick access to customer warranty status, ability to submit claims on behalf of customers | Operational user of customer profile and tracking modules |
| S05 | Product Manager | Owns specific product lines and has a vested interest in warranty data tied to product performance | Warranty claim rates by product, failure trends, data to inform product improvements | Consumer of reporting and analytics; no transactional system access |
| S06 | Business Sponsor / Executive Owner | Senior leader (e.g. VP of Operations or COO) funding and championing the project | ROI realisation, cost reduction, strategic alignment, project delivery on time and budget | Governance oversight; reviews executive-level reports |
| S07 | IT Project Manager | Manages the technical delivery of the system, coordinates between business and development teams | Scope control, timeline, resource allocation, risk management | Oversees end-to-end project delivery; not a day-to-day system user |
| S08 | System Architect / Lead Developer | Designs and builds the technical solution | System scalability, data integrity, security, maintainability, integration feasibility | Designs and develops the system; technical authority |
| S09 | QA / Test Engineer | Responsible for validating that the system meets functional and non-functional requirements | Test coverage, defect resolution, regression stability, UAT sign-off readiness | Tests all modules prior to go-live; no production system access |
| S10 | Data Analyst / BI Analyst | Responsible for warranty data reporting, KPI tracking, and business intelligence outputs | Data accuracy, availability of structured data, report configurability, export capability | Consumer of the reporting and admin dashboard modules |
| S11 | IT Security Officer | Ensures the system meets data protection, privacy, and cybersecurity standards | Customer PII protection, access controls, audit trails, compliance with data regulations (e.g. GDPR) | Reviews and approves security architecture; conducts periodic audits |
| S12 | System Administrator | Manages user accounts, system configuration, and operational health of the platform | Role-based access management, system uptime, configuration flexibility, audit logs | Admin-level user of the backend system |
| S13 | Legal / Compliance Officer | Ensures the system and its processes adhere to consumer protection laws and warranty regulations | Regulatory compliance, data retention policies, audit trail availability, terms and conditions management | Advisory stakeholder; reviews system outputs and documentation |
| S14 | Marketing Manager | Interested in customer engagement data and the post-purchase customer experience | Customer registration rates, opt-in data for communications, warranty expiry notification branding | Indirect stakeholder; may influence notification templates and customer-facing UI |
